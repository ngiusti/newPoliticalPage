Jane Smith for School Board

Biography

Jane Smith has served our community as a teacher, administrator, and community leader for nearly thirty years. Jane was born and raised in our community, received her bachelor’s degree in primary education from Western Oregon University in 1989 and a master’s degree in education from the University of Portland. As an elementary school teacher in our school district, Jane has spent her career helping our children achieve their potential as students, citizens, and future citizens. When Jane is not in her classroom, she likes to hike, read poetry, and spend time with her family. Jane lives in our community with her husband, Tom, her twin daughters, Joan and Susan, and their golden retriever Rover.

Issues

Jane Smith believes every student deserves a world-class education. That’s why, as our next School Board member, Jane will fight for robust funding for K-12 schools. She will aslo advocate for comprehensive, fact-based pedagogy according to the latest behavioral science. And she will empower parents and teachers to meet the individual needs of our kids. A vote for Jane is a vote for:

Class Sizes

More teaching assistants in our elementary schools, because every child deserves to have the one-on-one teaching she needs to succeed;

Newer Technology

Better computers in our middle school classrooms, because young people need to be comfortable learning on the technology they will use in the workforce

Career Guidence

Career and technical education (CTE) opportunities in our high schools, because every student should complete school with the skills, training, and know-how to thrive in today’s economy.
