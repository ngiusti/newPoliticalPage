Meet Susan!

Susan Thomas is the right choice for Madison County Judge. She was born and raised in McMinnville, earned her BA in English literature from Linfield College, and graduated summa cum laude from Willamette University College of Law. For more than twenty years, Susan has practiced law in Yamhill County, first as a public defender and later as a private practice attorney specializing in criminal defense. She has also served as a board member for the local YMCA, a youth mentor at McMinnville High School, and is a regular volunteer at the Yamhill County food bank. As our next judge, Susan will bring a lifetime of integrity, experience, and service to the bench.

On November 3, vote for Susan Thomas for Yamhill County Judge!

Judge Thomas Presiding
